Cruncifying-mint
September 30, 2013 [ sysadmin ] #linux Read in 2 min.

I got myself in a situation where I was forced to use mint. I missed Crunchbang and Openbox dearly right off the bat. So I decided to fix this. In order to get crunchbang specific functionality, you need their exectuable scripts. I copy them to my dropbox so I can just symlink them.

sudo apt-get install openbox dmenu tint2 terminator thunar wmctrl conky nitrogen clipit gtk-chtheme xscreensaver vim-gnome
ln $HOME/Dropbox/linux/crunchbang/openbox/* $HOME/.config/openbox
ln $HOME/Dropbox/linux/crunchbang/* $HOME/.config/
sudo chmod 755 /usr/bin/cb*
