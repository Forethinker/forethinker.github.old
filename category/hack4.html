Reinstall Laptop Screen Driver
September 23, 2013 [ hack ] Read in 1 min.

http://download.microsoft.com/download/1/1/f/11f7dd10-272d-4cd2-896f-9ce67f3e0240/devcon.exe http://support.microsoft.com/kb/311272

Once you extract, there are two folders, I386 and Ia64, which contains DevCon.exe. The archiecture depends on your cmd.exe instead of your machine.

devcon.exe --remove =monitor devcon.exe --update =monitor

Now I just put that into my auothotkey script and I am good to go. This is much better and simpler approach than the one that uses only auothotkey script

If you need more examples of DevCon, Microsoft provides them in their Dev Center page
