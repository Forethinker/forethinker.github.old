About Me

Hi! I am a former lifehacker fan with a bachelor degree in Computer Science. Now I enroll myself in various MOOCs and Stackexchange Q&A forums. I try to take learning and productivity to another level by seeking minimalism in possessions and limiting distractions with technology.

I record and index everything so I can focus. I endorse social network with a concrete purpose, so I closed my Facebook account long ago and I support anonymity on the internet. I filter internet with DD-WRT, iptables and OpenDNS, but I recommend K9 Web Protection to people whenever I can.

I take a walk when I am unable to focus and I also read Our Daily Bread every night so I can sleep better.

My introduction seems a bit choppy, but each sentence eventually will be linked to an article.

I recommend against subscribing to my RSS feed, because I update my articles constantly and you might end up reading my article twice.

I am also open to consulting. Email me with your concern and your offer.

If you found any of my article helpful, please help me stay motivated and get going by donating!
