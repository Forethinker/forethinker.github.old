Beats Solo 2.0 vs Other earphones
January 07, 2015 [ life ] #Earphones Read in 1 min.

I am listening to my favorite artist with Beats Solo 2.0 Wired On-Ear Headphones right now. It sounds great. Maybe it is because of the bigger cone, I can tell the dynamic range of the song and the bass is very lively. I have never been a fan of on-ear earphone because of their size. I am a minimalist and I like to keep things simple. Also my ears are sensitive to temperature and on-ear earphone does not help in that regard. The voice-cancelling earphones are good enough for me in most cases, and I tend to opt for LG Bluetooth earphones so that I don't have to worry about the wires. So I am debating whether I should sell or give away this headphone that I just received as a gift.
